    • Some articles related to Mathematical Olympiad

    When I was in high school, I was a student studying for Mathematical Olympiad. I won the first prize of The Examination for the National Senior High School’s Mathematical Competition in 2021.

    Most of the following content was written by me or with some friends when I was in high school. All these articles are in Chinese.

    Published on Mathematical New-Star Network(数学新星网):

    • The Problems and Solutions of 15th Sharygin Geometry Olympiad(第15届沙雷金几何奥林匹克试题与解析). Published in October 2019. This is my first (informal) paper.
    • The Problems and Solutions of 2020 Vietnam Mathematical Olympiad(2020越南数学奥林匹克试题解析). Published in March 2020.
    • The Problems and Solutions of 17th Sharygin Geometry Olympiad(第17届沙雷金几何奥林匹克试题与解析). Published in December 2021.
    • The Problems and Solutions of 8th Iranian Geometry Olympiad(第8届伊朗几何奥林匹克试题解析). Published in May 2022.
    • The Problems and Solutions of 18th Sharygin Geometry Olympiad(第18届沙雷金几何奥林匹克试题与解析). Published in January 2023.

    Published on High-School Mathematics(中等数学):

    • A Study on a Problem in American Team Selection Test(对一道美国国家队选拔考试题的探究). High-School Mathematics,2022,(03):15-17.This is my first formal paper.
    • Blatic Way Mathematical Contest in 2019(2019波罗的海之路数学竞赛). High-School Mathematics,2022,(07):35-41.

    (The High-School Mathematics journal is the only publication in China that focuses on reporting mathematics competitions.)
